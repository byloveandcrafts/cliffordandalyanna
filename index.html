How It All Began

A Tale of Destiny

By Alyanna  ·  Batangas City

It all began in Uptown back in 2018, where two ordinary days turned into something extraordinary. We were both just working then — two unsuspecting souls — not knowing that we were about to build a lifetime together.

A year later, our love blossomed into something even more beautiful as we welcomed our firstborn, baby boy Jaykkylle — our greatest blessing and the tender beginning of our little family.

"Two hearts, one story, a thousand moments"
— Clifford

Five years after, our hearts expanded once more with the arrival of our baby girl, Quinn — completing the love we never knew we needed.

Through the years, we have faced life hand in hand, growing, learning, and choosing each other every single day.
